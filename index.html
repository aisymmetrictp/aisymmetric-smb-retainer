Flexible Contracting

📅 Monthly Retainer Recommended

Flat monthly fee, 30-day cancellation. Sprint-based delivery with weekly calls and unlimited async. Start at $3K/mo.

🎯 Project-Based

Fixed scope, fixed price. Ideal for a defined AI build, automation, or integration. Scoped with a detailed SOW and delivered in 2–8 weeks. Starts at $10K. Cost-share models available for early-stage companies where AISymmetric co-invests in the build for a stake in future outcomes.

We put skin in the game. Reduced base retainer plus a percentage of revenue generated, costs saved, or equity stake in the business. Structures include revenue share (% of attributed revenue), cost-share (split build costs for shared IP), and equity partnerships for high-conviction engagements.
